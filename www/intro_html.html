WELCOME TO Hubs Bridge

A Web Application specifically designed for integration and analysis of your HubSpot data.

HubS bridge enables manual and automated data integration between your data sources and HubSpot to update and analyze your HubSpot object data such as contact, company, and deals. The integration relies on API calls made through a secured OAuth connection, please follow this link for more information about the secured connection: HubSpot OAuth Documentation

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH HubS Bridge:

  • Data integration via csv upload & Google Sheet, integration options are under development.
  • Download lists of contacts, deals, and companies with specific properties.
  • Run analysis using linear/logistic regressions to better understand your HubSpot data.
  • Better manage your sales leads by creating prediction to increase your conversion through leveraging the power of machine stewardship learning.
